# Version 1.0.1 — Stage 7 submission update

Released 18 July 2026.

## Corrected

- SIG034 is attributed to Zheng et al. (2021), DOI `10.1016/j.immuni.2021.03.002`, throughout the outward-facing release materials.
- Two-cohort and single-cohort sensitivity results are presented descriptively. The original frozen results remain preserved, but formal Hartung–Knapp confidence intervals and P values are not displayed for fewer than three contributing cohorts.

## Improved

- Outward-facing time labels use T24, T48, T72 and Day 5.
- The Supplementary Data workbook aligns with the Stage 7 reporting rules and terminology.
- Main TIFF files are RGB, 600 dpi, LZW-compressed and contain no alpha channel.
- Figure 1 uses a hatch to distinguish T48 bars in grayscale.
- Local usernames and workstation paths were removed from the public v1.0.1 package; the exact path-bound historical source copy remains available in the immutable v1.0.0 release.

## Scientific impact

No primary effect estimate, significance decision, heterogeneity estimate, prediction interval, gene-architecture classification or pathway conclusion changed.
